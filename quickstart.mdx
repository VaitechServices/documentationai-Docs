## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" title="Before You Begin">
Ensure you have:
- A valid email address for account creation
- Organization details ready (name, location, contact info)
- Web browser: Chrome, Firefox, or Safari (latest versions recommended)
</Callout>

## Create Your Free Trial Account

Sign up for a free trial to access all features without commitment.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Sign-Up Page" icon="external-link">
    Go to `https://app.wirecare.com/signup`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details">
    Provide your email, create a password, and select "Wildlife Rescue Organization" as account type.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email">
    Check your inbox for a verification link from WireCare and click it to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Onboarding">
    Answer basic questions about your organization size and primary focus (e.g., birds, mammals).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Log In and Set Up Your Organization

Access the dashboard and configure your team.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web Browser" icon="globe">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Log In">
        Navigate to `https://app.wirecare.com/login` and enter your credentials.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Organization Setup">
        Click "Set Up Organization" and enter name, address, and logo upload.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Mobile App" icon="smartphone">
    Download from App Store or Google Play, then scan QR code or enter credentials.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Complete Your First Animal Intake

Record a new rescue to start tracking care.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Animals" icon="paw">
    From dashboard, click "Animals" > "New Intake".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill Intake Form">
    Enter details:
    
    | Field          | Example Value             |
    |----------------|---------------------------|
    | Species        | Bald Eagle                |
    | ID             | EAG-456                   |
    | Intake Date    | 2024-10-15                |
    | Location Found | Forest coordinates: 40.7128° N, 74.0060° W |
    
    Add initial health notes and photos.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign Tasks">
    Generate daily feeding and medication checklists automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and Notify">
    Save record; team gets notified for care assignments.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Add Your First Volunteer

Quickly onboard a volunteer for shifts.

<Expandable title="Volunteer Sign-Up Steps" default-open="true">
  1. Go to "Volunteers" > "Invite New".
  2. Enter name, email, phone, and skills (e.g., bird handling).
  3. Set availability and send invite link.
  4. Volunteer completes profile via email link.
</Expandable>

<Callout kind="tip" title="Pro Tip">
Customize your dashboard notifications in Settings to get real-time alerts for intakes and shift sign-ups.
</Callout>
