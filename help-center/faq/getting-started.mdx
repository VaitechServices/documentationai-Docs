## Overview

To begin using Vaitech Services:

1. Sign up at `https://dashboard.vaitechservices.com`.
2. Verify your email address.
3. Login using your credentials.
4. Click "Create Project" on your dashboard homepage.
5. Follow the on-screen setup instructions.

## Tips

- Check your email spam folder for verification mail.
- Use the Community page if you have issues during signup.
