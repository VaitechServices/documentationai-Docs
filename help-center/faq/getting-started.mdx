## Getting started FAQ

<Callout kind="info">
  New WireCare customers need access to the dashboard, a valid work email address, and an up-to-date browser such as Chrome, Edge, or Firefox.
</Callout>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Keep your email inbox open while you sign up so you can complete verification straight away and avoid delays accessing the dashboard.
</Callout>

<ExpandableGroup>

  <Expandable title="How do I sign up for WireCare?">

  Go to `https://dashboard.vaitechservices.com` and select the option to create a new WireCare account. Enter your work email address, set a secure password, and confirm any required organisation details.

  After you submit the form, WireCare sends a verification email to the address you used for signup.

  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="How do I verify my email address?">

  Open the verification email from WireCare and click the verification link in the message. This confirms you own the email address and activates your dashboard access.

  When verification succeeds, the browser redirects you back to the WireCare dashboard login page.

  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="What if the verification email does not arrive?">

  First, check your spam or junk folder for the WireCare message and move it back to your inbox if needed. Then refresh your inbox and wait a few minutes, as delivery can be delayed by some mail providers.

  If you still cannot see the email, try sending it again from the signup screen or the dashboard prompt. If repeated attempts fail, contact WireCare support so the team can confirm your account details and help you complete verification.

  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="How do I log in to the WireCare dashboard?">

  Once you have verified your email, return to `https://dashboard.vaitechservices.com`. Enter the email address and password you used during signup, then select the option to log in.

  If the credentials are correct, you land on the WireCare dashboard home, where you can view or create projects.

  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="How do I create my first project in WireCare?">

  From the WireCare dashboard home, look for the **Create Project** button, usually in the top-right corner. Select it to open the new project form, then add a project name and choose any default settings.

  Confirm the form to create the project. The project appears on your dashboard, and you can select it to start managing your WireCare configuration.

  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Where can I find a step-by-step Create Project guide?">

  If you want more detailed instructions, follow the dedicated [Create Project guide](/help-center/guides/create-project). It walks you through each field in the form and shows what to expect once the project is created.

  Use that guide alongside this FAQ if you are setting up WireCare for the first time and want extra confidence you have configured everything correctly.

  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Where do I get help if I am blocked getting started?">

  If you are stuck on signup, verification, or your first login, review this FAQ to confirm you have followed each step. For persistent login or access issues, see the [Cannot Access Dashboard](/help-center/troubleshooting/cannot-access-dashboard) troubleshooting article.

  When you still cannot resolve the problem, reach out to WireCare support from within the dashboard help menu (if you can log in) or via your usual WireCare support channel so the team can investigate your account.

  </Expandable>

</ExpandableGroup>
