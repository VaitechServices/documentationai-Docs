---
title: Cannot Access Dashboard
description: Resolve access issues with Vaitech dashboard.
---

## Symptoms

- Can't login to dashboard
- "Access Denied" or blank page shown

## Possible Causes

- Incorrect credentials
- Account not verified
- Browser cache issues

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check credentials">
    Confirm your username and password are correct.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify account">
    Make sure you have clicked the verification link sent to your email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Try logging in from a private/incognito window, or clear cache.
  </Step>
</Steps>
