## Overview

WireCare empowers wildlife rehabilitation organizations with a comprehensive SaaS platform. You streamline operations, manage volunteers efficiently, track animal care meticulously, and coordinate rescue efforts—all from one intuitive dashboard. Designed specifically for wildlife welfare, WireCare reduces administrative burden so you focus on what matters: rescuing and rehabilitating animals.

Track animals from intake to release, schedule volunteer shifts seamlessly, generate financial reports, and access a centralized resource library. With mobile-friendly tools and automated checklists, WireCare scales with your organization, whether you're a small rescue team or managing multiple sites.

## Quick Start

Get up and running in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Visit `https://app.wirecare.com` and create your free account. No credit card required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Your Organization" icon="building">
    Enter basic details about your rescue operation, including location and team size.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Your First Animal" icon="paw">
    Use the intake form to log details like species, condition, and intake date.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite Volunteers" icon="users">
    Send invites via email and set up your first shift roster.
  </Step>
</Steps>
