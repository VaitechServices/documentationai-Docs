## Overview

WireCare centralises day-to-day operations for wildlife rehabilitation teams. You record animal intakes, coordinate volunteer shifts, track care tasks and observations, and keep finance records in a single web app.

The app includes dedicated modules for animal records, care workflows, roster scheduling, branch and location management, transactions, and reference resources. Field-focused tools like the Foliage Map and Possum Helper support decision-making on site while role-based access control keeps sensitive data protected.

<Callout kind="info" collapsed="false">
  WireCare uses Supabase authentication with role-based access. New users must confirm their email address and be accepted by a Coordinator before accessing the dashboard and data modules.
</Callout>

## Quick start

Follow this path to get from first registration to working with core modules.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Register an account" icon="user-plus" title-type="p">
    Go to the <a href="https://app.wirecare.com.au/register" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Register</a> page and create a new user with your email address and password.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm your email" icon="mail" title-type="p">
    Check your inbox and complete the email confirmation link. WireCare enforces email confirmation before granting access.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign in and open the dashboard" icon="log-in" title-type="p">
    After confirmation, visit `/login` to sign in. On success, the app redirects you to `/dashboard` with an overview of available modules.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add your first animal intake" icon="paw-print" title-type="p">
    Navigate to `/animals/add` to create an animal record. Capture key intake information so it appears in the `/animals` list.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set up an initial roster" icon="users" title-type="p">
    Open `/roster` to review or create upcoming shifts, aligning volunteer availability with current and future intake demands.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip" collapsed="false">
  Move on to the [Quickstart](/quickstart) guide for a more detailed walkthrough of configuration and everyday workflows.
</Callout>
